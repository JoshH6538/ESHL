Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We do not collect any personal information from visitors to this website.

3. Form Submissions & Email

If you choose to contact us using the website, your email client handles the message. No content is stored or captured by the website itself.

4. Changes to This Policy

Should we ever change how we handle data, this page will be updated to reflect those changes.
